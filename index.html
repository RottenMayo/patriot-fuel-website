Mission

What We Do

Patriot Fuel Industrial Supply Co. is a specialized procurement company serving military bases and government contractors in the fuel systems sector. We don't just ship parts — we source the right parts, verified against the correct specifications, so your project passes inspection and stays on schedule.

Operating under the RMMR program and similar DoD maintenance frameworks, we bridge the gap between service order and completed invoice — handling compliance verification, specification matching, and sourcing so your field crew has exactly what they need.

About

Who We Are

Patriot Fuel Industrial Supply Co. was founded by Matthew Maness, a government contracting professional with hands-on experience in DoD fuel system maintenance programs — including direct work under the RMMR (Repair, Maintenance, Minor Renovation) program at military installations.

Matthew knows this work from the inside. He has written scopes of work, managed subcontractor specifications, sourced compliant materials, and invoiced the government directly. That experience is the foundation of every procurement we handle — we don't source blind, we source to spec.

Based in Colorado and serving military installations and government contractors globally.
